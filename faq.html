To unlink a seller official account and link it to a marketing account,
enabling a basket showcase in
TikTok Shop, follow these concise steps:

  1. Log in to your TikTok Ads Manager account.
  2. Click "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu in the upper right corner of the page.
  3. Choose "Linked Accounts" in the left-hand menu.
  4. Find the seller official account you want to unlink and click "Unlink."
  5. Click "Link a new account" and select "Marketing Account."
  6. Follow the prompts to link your marketing account.
  7. With your marketing account linked, you can create a basket showcase in TikTok Shop.
